Women’s Empowerment

Dr. Padmavathi Ammaiyar founded Tamilaga Magalir Peravai with a vision to empower women and create meaningful social change across Tamil Nadu. Through her dedication to women's welfare, education, rural development, environmental protection, and community service, she continues to inspire thousands of women to become confident leaders and active contributors to society. Her mission is to build a future where every woman has the opportunity to grow, succeed, and uplift her community.
