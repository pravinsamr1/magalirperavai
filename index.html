About Us

Empowering Women, Strengthening Communities

Tamil Nadu Women's Federation is a social and political movement dedicated to empowering women and creating sustainable community development. We work towards women's economic independence, rural progress, environmental protection, skill development, and social welfare. Our mission is to build a stronger and self-reliant Tamil Nadu through collective action and inclusive growth.

Founder & President

Dr. Padmavathi Ammaiyar – A Visionary Leader for Women's Empowerment

Dr. Padmavathi Ammaiyar founded Tamilaga Magalir Peravai with a vision to empower women and create meaningful social change across Tamil Nadu. Through her dedication to women's welfare, education, rural development, environmental protection, and community service, she continues to inspire thousands of women to become confident leaders and active contributors to society. Her mission is to build a future where every woman has the opportunity to grow, succeed, and uplift her community.

A Stronger Future for Women and Communities

Our vision is to create a progressive Tamil Nadu where women are empowered and every citizen has access to opportunities for growth and development.

  • Equal opportunities for women in all sectors
  • Self-reliant and developed rural communities
  • Sustainable environmental and energy solutions

Driving Positive Change Through Collective Action

Our mission is to uplift women, strengthen rural communities, and promote sustainable development through education, entrepreneurship.

  • Improve rural infrastructure and livelihoods
  • Encourage solar energy and green initiatives
  • Advance organic farming and natural resource protection

Lighting the Lamp in Every Woman’s Life

The 5-thiri kuthu vilakku can symbolize the five major strengths every woman carries within her.

    Meaning of the 5 Flames
  • Knowledge – Education & awareness
  • Courage – Strength to face challenges
  • Self-Respect – Dignity and confidence
  • Compassion – Care for family & society
  • Leadership – Women leading Tamil Nadu forward
