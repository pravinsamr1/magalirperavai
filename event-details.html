Women's Empowerment Workshop

Tamilaga Magalir Peravai organises regular workshops and programmes designed to empower women across Tamil Nadu. Our events focus on leadership development, financial independence, skill building and community outreach. Every programme is an opportunity to learn, grow and inspire others around you.
